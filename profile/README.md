## What We're Building

InfraVillage is the ecosystem platform behind **DraftMyStack**, our flagship cloud cost modeling tool.

We're building infrastructure intelligence tools that help professionals make better architectural and cost decisions.

**DraftMyStack is our first product.** The broader InfraVillage platform will support additional infrastructure decision-making capabilities as the ecosystem matures.

---

## Platform Vision

InfraVillage exists to solve a simple problem: **infrastructure knowledge is fragmented**.

Cost modeling tools lack template libraries. Infrastructure patterns are scattered across blogs, GitHub repos, and StackOverflow. There's no clear path to monetize infrastructure expertise beyond consulting.

**We believe:**
- Infrastructure patterns should be shareable and discoverable
- Template creators deserve to monetize their expertise
- Cost awareness should be built into architectural decisions
- Community knowledge should be accessible, not scattered

InfraVillage will eventually provide a space for battle-tested templates, cost-aware patterns, and community-driven infrastructure knowledge.

**But not yet.** Platforms are earned, not launched.
